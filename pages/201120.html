ENGL 1127
Students planning to enrol in a first-semester English course must take the Langara English Test (LET) or Language Proficiency Index Test (LPI) prior to the semester in which they want to register. Students who have BC English 12 or BC English Literature 12 or equivalent with an 80% or higher are not required to write the LET or LPI.

Students not meeting those exemptions need the following scores on LET or LPI:
LET 3 or LPI 26 on the essay with one of the following:
5/10 or higher in sentence structure; or
5/10 or higher in English usage; or
10/20 or higher in reading comprehension.

Students who have scored a level 3 on the LET (or 26/40 with a 5 or higher in English usage on the LPI) are also strongly encouraged to register in English 1121 (there is no tuition charge for this course for Canadian Citizens and Permanent residents) in order to upgrade their reading and writing skills. English 1121 will help students at this prerequisite level succeed in ENGL 1127 which is the university transfer course.
